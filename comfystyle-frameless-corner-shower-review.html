A 44 to 48 inch range means you aren't locked into ordering a door that only works if your corner measures exactly one number, which is the situation you face with the DreamLine French Corner 34's fixed 34.5 by 34.5 inch footprint.

Price also works in its favor. At $599.99, you're paying less than the Woodbridge and well under the DreamLine, while still getting a frameless look that reads as more modern than a heavily trimmed corner door. Frequently Asked Questions

What size corner does the ComfyStyle Frameless Corner Shower Enclosure fit?

The ComfyStyle Frameless Corner Shower Enclosure adjusts to fit a 44 to 48 inch wide corner opening with a 34 inch depth and 72 inch height, so measure your corner in both directions before ordering to confirm it falls inside that adjustable range.

Is the ComfyStyle Frameless Corner Shower Enclosure a good value compared to other corner shower doors?

At $599.99, the ComfyStyle costs $15.13 less than the Woodbridge and $37.50 less than the DreamLine French Corner 34, while covering a wider 44 to 48 inch adjustable footprint than either of those fixed-size alternatives. It costs $15 more than the ELEGANT, the cheapest option in this comparison.

Does the ComfyStyle Frameless Corner Shower Enclosure include the shower base?

No. Like the other enclosures in this review, the ComfyStyle ships as the glass and frame assembly only, so you supply your own shower pan, base, or tiled curb underneath.

Final Verdict

It comes down to flexibility against a fixed price. At $599.99, the ComfyStyle Frameless Corner Shower Enclosure undercuts the Woodbridge and DreamLine while offering a 44 to 48 inch adjustable width that neither of those fixed-size doors can match. If your corner opening doesn't land on one clean measurement, that range alone makes ComfyStyle worth the $15 premium over the ELEGANT. If your corner already matches a fixed size and budget is the deciding factor, the ELEGANT remains the cheaper way to solve the same problem. Either way, measure your corner in both directions before you order, since none of these four enclosures leave room to adjust after installation.
